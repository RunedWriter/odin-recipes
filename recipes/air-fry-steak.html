Air Fried Steak

Perfect air fryer steak that's seared on the ouside and juice on the inside!

Save yourself the splatter and mess of conventional frying and enjoy a delicous air fried steak fresh from your kitchen! Cook time will vary but assuming you are cooking a 1 in thick steak it's going to be...

  • Medium Steak: 12 minutes in a preheated air fryer (flip it at six minutes).
  • Medium Rare: 10 minutes in a preheated air fryer (flip it at five minutes).
  • Rare: 8 minutes in a preheated air fryer (flip it at four minutes).
  • Well done: No. Bad. Stop. Go back! (14 minutes in a preheated air fryer (flip it at 7 minutes))

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp hosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 sirloin steaks, 1 in thick
  • olive oil spray

Steps

  1. Combine the spices in a small bowl.
  2. Spray the steak with olive oil and coat both sides with the spices.
  3. Preheat the air fryer so the basket gets hot. For a 1-inch steak, air fry 400F 10 minutes turning halfway, for medium rare, for medium, cook 12 minutes, flipping halfway. See temp chart below, time may vary slightly with different air fryer models, and the thickness of the steaks.
  4. Finish with a pinch of more salt and black pepper.
  5. Let it rest, tented with foil 5 minutes before slicing.
  6. Enjoy!
